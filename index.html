Swahilipot Hub

SPH is a community space that nurtures youth talent and skills in technology, creatives/arts and heritage. It is a representation of the past (heritage) and future (digital)

On this day, I volunteered to visit Little Sister Home For The Poor at Tudor Mombasa a program organized by a group of youths by the name Reach For Purpose in partnership with SwahiliPot Hub. It was a lovely experience as we got to interact with the old that have been abandoned and some that come from poor families that cannot cater for their needs, and made them feel loved and cared for.

Baus Taka Enterprise

Baus taka is a tech integrated solid waste management company. Its main objective is to provide quality, reliable and eco–friendly waste management solutions to residential, corporate and commercial institutions with the aim of conserving and sustaining the environment.


On this day we had gone to see the plastic waste collection centre located at Old Town Mombasa near Fort Jesus and to also do a community cleanup and see how plastic wastes are seperated, cleaned and shredded into smallest particles for easy recycling.

Mission Relief

It was on December 2022 when there was drought in most parts of our country Kenya. Mission Relief UK in partnership with Mission Relief Africa had a program for food distribution in parts of the country that had been affected and I took part in volunteering on the program. We went all the way to Bamba which is in Kilifi County to take foodstuff to the people of the community that were most affected. It was really emotional to see families with no hopes for food,with the environment being sooo dull and dry and the sun so hot.
